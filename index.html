My Wife and I are somewhat adverse to risk, so doing this in the first place was out of our comfort zone. Unable to secure a space at the railway station I came across this site and found a space literally a couple of minutes away on foot. I read the reviews and what the owner had put, it really looked like he was trying, and I was a bit impressed by the whole entrepreneurialism of this guy. Running out of options I booked it. I contacted the owner (Rohit) via txt, he soon replied, and I felt better about the whole idea. He gave good directions and had a warm and friendly approach. It was agreed that he would call me a few days before to answer any questions and if I would like to take advantage of any of the other services he had to offer such as a valet service and the like. I declined the extras (one step at a time for me). I found the place on the day without much trouble, and gave up my car key to a member of his family so it could be moved as necessary and left for London. On my way back, I received a text from Rohit offering to collect me and my family from the station simply as a courtesy! I could not believe this man, who had earned practically nothing out of me was prepared to do this! My train was late going down, (no fault of operator), Travel Lodge was disappointing (some staff and breakfast), but this guy was in fact the best thing about our traveling experience, lovely man, so to was his family, just really warm and friendly and I would like to thank Rohit for a great first class offer, I would not even consider parking at the station ever again. Can’t recommend him more, (but I would if I could). Thanks once again, Steve.
