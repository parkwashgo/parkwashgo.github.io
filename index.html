Preston's #1 Parking spot

We are a well-established business, offering a highly professional Parking and Valet service for over 8 years now. We are proud to say we are Preston City’s Number 1 most used private parking space for Preston (Lancs) Railway Station.

  • The parking space is an easy 5 minute walk to Preston (Lancs) Railway Station
  • A newly installed driveway with block paving and a dropped kerb provides easy access.
  • It is very spacious and can accommodate up to 8 vehicles at any one time
  • It is the nearest private parking to Preston (Lancs) Railway Station.
  • State of the art Solar paving lighting.
  • Ultra-modern 24Hr CCTV system for your security.
  • Very active Neighbourhood Watch in operation.
  • Transfers to and from Preston Rail Station (for a small fee) Subject to availability.
  • Premium Car Wash and Valet service
